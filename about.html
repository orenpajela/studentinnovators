About

Taking for students by students to the next level.

Innovator: A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.

Student Innovators is a student organization that's taking "for students, by students" to the next level through innovation. Everything that this organization does is ideated, planned, and executed by students just like you. And the best part is, we're a student collective where we share our knowledge with others so that together we can accomplish anything.

Smash the Status Quo

We're a student community that wants a better future for all of us. You have ideas, goals, and places that you want to be. We do too, and we believe that we can get there by helping each other out and by working towards a common mission.

And the mission?
To connect our campus community through collaboration in order to create awesome things.

To connect, collaborate, and create in a constant way, where creating awesome things leads to more connection and collaboration, which leads to creating more awesome things, which leads to connection, then collaboration... So our mission is pretty much just an infinite loop of awesomeness. Awesome.

The SI Process

It Always Start With You

At SI, we know that learning about yourself, discovering what you want, and setting your own goals is the beginning of your journey. Only you can start it.

Experiment Always

Nothing beats learning through experience, and constantly experimenting in projects will help us learn and grow. Failures are a just as much of a win as successes.

Connect and Collaborate

Sometimes it's simply about who you know. We always look for positive ways to participate in our communities to network with others doing the same.

Build Yourself, Build Others

We understand that the reason we invest in ourselves is so that we can invest in others, in our communities, and in the world to make a brighter future for everyone.

Let's talk about creating
awesome things...

This website was built to be a UTD exclusive platform to help you get connected in three ways- GROUPS, EVENTS, and LABS.
  • Join or create your own group to find new people with the same interests.
  • Find or start your own event to bring people together.
  • Experiment in a collaborative Lab and challenge yourself with projects.
We're inviting you to be a part of our community and to build with us, whether it be your own group or event or working on something in one of our labs. This is literally just the beginning, and we have plans to create even more awesome things that will help us learn and develop both as people and as professionals.
