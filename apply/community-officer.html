Community Officer - Description

About Student Innovators

Student Innovators is a new student organization at UT Dallas with the goal of connecting students of different backgrounds in order to accomplish awesome things. We are a team of movers and shakers who back up crazy ideas with decisive action. We are big picture thinkers, big risk takers, and big problem solvers. We are social entrepreneurs, computer nerds, fitness junkies, creative designers, almost-main-stage musicians, and more. We enjoy music festivals, video games, artisan crafts, the internet, and listing a bunch of things in a row. Our organization values connection, collaboration, and community, and we believe that our strength comes from the belief that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.

Student Innovators has a mission to be the student organization with the biggest positive impact at UTD. We are a hybrid organization- both a 501c3 non-profit and a UTD recognized student organization, and we grow and develop our members to become change-makers in their campus, communities, and companies by offering one-of-a-kind experiences that will revolutionize their career trajectory.

Job Description

We are looking for motivated students to join the SI team as a Community Officer and become a driving organizational force to help accomplish the goals of Student Innovators. You will work within the Community team to recruit, manage, and engage members to help our mission of cultivating a more connected campus. You’ll look to find and create value through relationships with professors, other organizations, and the community abroad. You’ll work closely with other SI Leadership and team members to foster a culture of boundless creativity and continuous improvement. Careers that this position can prepare you for include HR Manager, Talent Acquisition, Community Manager, Strategic Partnerships Executive, Business Development Manager, and more.

Responsibilities

You will be working with a team of like-minded students to maintain operations and oversee responsibilities of the Community Team. These responsibilities include:

  • Develop a strategy to recruit new members and a retention plan for current members.
  • Function as an advisor to each division regarding allocation of members and any organizational or standards issues.
  • Maintain a deep understanding and current awareness of UT Dallas students, events, and happenings.
  • Look for creative ways for Student Innovators and it’s members to be involved both on campus and in the community abroad.
  • Maintain a dynamic list of all members and find opportunities to increase the value to our members.
  • Works closely with each SI division to encourage diversity and inclusion on all projects.
  • Holds a new member orientation every semester, educating new members on the culture, structure, and opportunities within SI.

Requirements

You’ll feel at home in this position if:

  • You are a problem solver with big aspirations for the future who strives to leave positive impacts in your wake.
  • You are a leader who is comfortable with managing team members and also escalating situations when needed.
  • You are professional and presentable and you enjoy connecting with other student leaders, professors, and local organizations/businesses.
  • You are a visionary who enjoys creating value through relationships and is able to think outside the box when structuring partnerships.
  • You’re a planner and an organizer who is capable of knowing and figuring out a place for everything.
  • You are curious and you enjoy researching and learning about things you didn’t know.
  • You feel at home with Google Sheets spreadsheets and the rest of the Google apps suite, with an ability to create killer proposals.
