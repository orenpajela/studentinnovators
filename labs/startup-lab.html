Startup Lab

Collaboration, Experimentation, Innovation.

Build something
from nothing

The Startup Lab is a major-agnostic, cross-disciplinary, multi-organizational collection of UTD students who are are committed to learning about (and actually) starting new businesses.

If you're looking for a group of students with dreams as big as yours, then you've found the right place.

Join the Lab
Join the Lab

We run experiments, projects, and initiatives in:

  • Pitching
  • Deck Building
  • Value Propositions
  • Customer Segments
  • Feasibility Studies
  • Market Research & Validation
  • MVP Building
  • Organization Theory
  • Business Model Canvas
  • Equity Structures
  • Revenue Generation
  • Fundraising & Investing
And anything else we want to to do.

Want to join our Lab community?

You have big dreams and big ambitions. You want to solve some of the world's biggest problems and you want to do it through entrepreneurship. You want to be challenged, you want to take the road less traveled, and you want to build something out of nothing. Well we have good news: So do we.

The Startup Lab is designed to connect you with like-minded entrepreneurs and go-getters to collaborate with so that you can build awesome things. We want to make things happen, we want to build things, and we want to make an impact within our communities in a positive way.

Sound like something you want to be a part of? Come join the Startup Lab today.

Startup Lab Resources

Check out this curated list of resources to help you learn about and explore the avant-garde world of startups.

Learn About a
Lean Startup

This is a free course you can take in order to learn more about the Lean Startup methodology, including hypothesis testing, experiment designs, and MVP building.

Slidebean: Successful
Pitch Deck Examples

Hone your pitch deck by analyzing and learning from startups who have found success. Includes decks from Airbnb, Uber, Facebook, and more.

Kevin Hale- How to Pitch Your Startup

Y Combinator Partner Kevin Hale shares his tips and tricks on how to craft your pitch in a clear and concise way that will appeal to potential investors.

Wealth Inequality in America

This YouTube video demonstrates a pressing problem in today's socio-economic landscape. As entrepreneurs, this video challenges you to answer the most important question: What is your *why*?

It's Time To Build
by Marc Andreessen

Marc Andreessen sounds the horn in a call-to-action for entrepreneurs, investors, and innovators alike to smash through the status quo of American complacency and start doing what made America the best: Building.

The Lesson to Unlearn
by Paul Graham

Paul Graham presents an intriguing premise to students and aspiring entrepreneurs in this essay. Whether you relate to the problem or not, this essay provides an interesting perspective to take into account.
