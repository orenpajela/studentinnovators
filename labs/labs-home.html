Labs

Where the magic happens.

Welcome SI Labs: The unrestricted playground for ultimate learning and experimentation. We'll run projects, experiments, and initiatives every single day with a mission to discover groundbreaking innovation, feed our curiosity, and gain valuable skills and experiences in the fields/topics we care about. We also do it together: Learning, teaching, and doing as a community of builders and experimenters making leaps towards the best versions of ourselves.

Virtual Friendly

Any UTD student can participate in Lab projects and initiatives online. We utilize the virtual tools and technology available to make sure that students who are remote or have a chance to involved and engaged.

Project Based

Lab student projects and initiatives are pitched by you, supported and funded by SI, and run by Team Leads. We want you to look back and be proud of what you've built, created, and accomplished.

Innovate Together

Our labs are made up of students of all different backgrounds and majors and from multiple organizations across UTD who are committed to collaborative learning by experimentation.
