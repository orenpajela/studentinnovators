Design Lab

Experimentation, Collaboration, Innovation.

Create something
incredible

The Design Lab is a major-agnostic, cross-disciplinary, multi-organizational collection of UTD students who are excited by design, creative practice, and everything in between.

If you're looking for a group of students with dreams as big as yours, then you've found the right place.

Join the Lab
Join the Lab

We run experiments, projects, and initiatives in:

  • UI/UX Design
  • Product Design
  • Website Design/Development
  • Interior Design
  • 3D Modeling
  • Hardware Architecture
  • Worldbuilding
  • Video Games
  • Character Design
  • Typography
  • Color Theory
  • Fashion
And anything else we want to to do.

Want to join our Lab community?

So you're a person who sees the beauty in design. You see how design permeates society, life, and the world around you. You get excited when you see ingenious design, and you scoff at terrible design. You're constantly rethinking how products and experiences could be better, and you believe you could make it better because you're an innovator.

If you're that person, then you'll find other people just like you in the Design Lab. You'll collaborate with them to learn more about design and to hone your craft, so that ultimately you can leave your mark on the world and on society with your own creations. At Student Innovators, we're inviting you to be a part of our community.

Come build with us.
