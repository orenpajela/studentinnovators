Music Lab

Innovation, Experimentation, Collaboration.

The language that
we all understand

The music lab is a major agnostic, cross-discipline, multi-organizational collection of UTD students who have a driving passion for all things music and sound.

We're committed to learning whatever we can about music to gain an understanding and appreciation for the art and sharing it with others.

We run experiments, projects, and initiatives in:

  • Ensemble Creation
  • Music Theory
  • Musical Instruments
  • Composition and Production
  • Sound Design
  • Sound Mixing/Mastering
  • Audio Engineering
  • Acoustical Engineering
  • Professional Audio
  • DJ/VJ
  • Foley
  • PRACTICING!!
And anything else we want to to do.

Want to join our Lab community?

Music is a world within itself, and everyone knows how awesome that world is. It's a form of art that's everywhere and has impacted society since the beginning of time. The infinite world of music is one that we'd like to explore, whether it be fiddling with synthesizers or starting a cover band with other students.

Whether you're wanting to pursue a career in music or you're just passionate about music as a hobby, we'll create opportunities for us to learn about, create, and share music and musical discoveries. You'll find other people who love music just as much as you do and have the chance to collaborate with us in whatever ways you imagine.

Jam out with us.
