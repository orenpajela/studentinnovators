Data Lab

Experimentation, Collaboration, Innovation.

Empower decisions with information

The Data Lab is a major-agnostic, cross-disciplinary, multi-organizational collection of UTD students who are committed to understanding how data can be used to interpret humanity.

We collect, analyze, and decipher data in ways that we find exciting and interesting.

Join the Lab
Join the Lab

We run experiments, projects, and initiatives in:

  • Survey Design
  • Social Experiments
  • Behavior Informatics
  • Data Analytics
  • Data Visualization
  • Web Scraping
  • Strategic Planning
  • Independent Research
  • Statistical Analysis
  • Machine Learning/AI
  • Data Mining
  • Business Intelligence
And anything else we want to to do.

Want to join our Lab community?

The technological and industrial revolutions centered around data is real. Companies, organizations, and countries around the world are learning how to use data in incredible ways and for unbelievable purposes. Data is the new oil but even more influential and powerful, and in order to compete in today's society, understanding and learning how to use data is a necessity.

That's what we're committed to learning here at the Data Lab. We understand that even in the microcosm of UTD there is so much information to learn from. We collaborate with each other to design surveys and conduct our own research so that we can empower our decisions with empirical evidence.
