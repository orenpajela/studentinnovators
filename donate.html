A letter to our potential supporters:

To the potential supporters of Student Innovators:
‍
You're received this letter because we have reason to believe that you might be interested in supporting our mission. At its core, the mission of our organization is simple: to connect our campus communities through collaboration so that students can create awesome things.
Connect, Collaborate, Create.
It's a simple mission that's rooted in a need to create and innovate, and we are proud of what we've done so far. Everything that you see here coded on this website: the designs, illustrations, the interactions and more, was created by our members. And we have a lot that we want to do.
The great thing about being students is that not only do we have a lot that we want to do, it's that we know we can do it. We are future engineers, programmers, policy makers, business leaders and more. So if we want to create anything, whether it be an app or a website, a new restaurant, or even a flamethrower, we have the talent at our schools to do that.
"But why?" you may ask. 
"Because why not?" We'll reply.
We are students with big dreams and ambitions, and we're smart enough to know that in order to reach these big dreams, we have to take little steps. And that's what this organization is about. Little steps and little improvements that over time will add up to big leaps and big innovations.
If you've read this far, we'd like to invite you to be a part of our what we're doing by supporting us through your donations. Every dollar goes directly to support student projects, student initiatives, and student dreams. It's an investment into our future and our communities future. Our mission is only made possible through people like you, and we hope that you could support us.
Help us do some awesome things. We're counting on you.
Sincerely,
The Student Innovators Team
