About Us

Who we are:

Student Innovators is a UTD student organization and non-profit with a mission to create awesome things by connecting and collaborative with our campus community. We're an organized group of student innovators, doers, and thinkers who collaborate on individual and community projects to encourage working and learning together through experimentation, hands-on projects, and innovative ideas.

What we do:

We are builders and creators. Right on this website, we've built a platform and an organization that's designed to support your imagination and ambition: whatever you'd like to do, we'll help you do it. Want to find new people and friends? Check out our GROUPS. Bored at quarantine and curious if there's any virtual events going on? Head over to EVENTS. Looking to get serious with your work and start honing your skills? Do it in our LABS. We're inviting you to come build, create, and innovate with your UTD community.

Why we do it:

We believe that if we bring UTD students together through innovation, we can solve big problems and do amazing things. We're committed to making change and leaving positive impacts on our communities, and that anything is possible by organizing together.

Big Thinkers

If you want to solve the world's biggest challenges, you're looking in the right place. As students, we're aware of the vital role we have in tackling issues like climate change, inequality, injustice, and more.

Growth Focused

We want our all of our members to feel confident and prepared to meet academic goals, career aspirations, and life's challenges head on. We strive together every single day to be the best versions of ourselves.

Virtual Friendly

In the midst of a global pandemic, we're organizing in a virtual-first approach. All UTD students can be involved with Student Innovators whether they're taking classes online or if they live on campus.
